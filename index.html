About

Hello, my name is Anne Spitler-Kashuba living in the beautiful redwood forest region of Sonoma County, California.I'm a frontend developer.

I began my professional life in merchandising and logo design. Transitioned to working with children with communication and literacy difficulties as speech-language pathologist. Now, I am glad to be bringing all of my knowledge and experiences to building websites and helping organizations communicate with their community.

My aim is to help teams create and maintain websites that are well-crafted from the inside out. Clear, responsive code behind the scenes & accessible content onscreen.

I am looking for a diverse team whose members enjoy the creative process, appreciate how differing approaches and perspectives can make projects even stronger, and value knowledge-sharing and life-long learning.

I bring a diverse set of abilities developed while working in the healthcare and education settings, combined with a life-long passion for the arts and making.

My jam: making and enjoying tasty food, learning languages, collecting textiles, and making art.

Personal goal: visiting as many fantastic bakeries as possible around the world. (If you have bakery recommendations to share, please do!)
